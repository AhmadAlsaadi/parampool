This application computes the motion of a ball with radius R and mass m under the influence of gravity, air resistance (drag) and lift because of a given spinrate. The motion starts with a prescribed initial_velocity making an angle initial_angle with the ground. A wind velocity, positive in positive x direction, can also be given (w). Many numerical methods can be used to solve the equations of motion. Some legal names are ForwardEuler, RK2, RK4, and Fehlberg (adaptive Runge-Kutta 4/5 order). If the timestep dt is None, approximately 500 steps are used, but dt can also be given a desired float value.

The boolean variable plot_simplified_motion adds the curve of the motion without drag and lift (the standard parabolic trajectory). This curve helps illustrate the effect of drag and lift. When new_plot is false (unchecked), the new computed curves are added to the previous ones since last time new_plot was true.
